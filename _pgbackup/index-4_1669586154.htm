Affiliate Program

How does our affiliate program work?

Our affiliate program works by simple principles and it's in 3 levels of 5-2-1%.
You have to be an active member (a member with a deposit) to earn it.
All you have to do is share our project idea with others,
via sharing your affiliate link available in your personal account.

  • We pay commission from any investment made by your referred person.

  • You will receive commission of all deposit volumes made by your sub-referrals.

  • Receive commission of all deposit volumes from third level referrals.
